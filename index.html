Welcome to MJ Lilly Associates

MJ Lilly Associates, LLC is a strategic communications consultancy. We are not just marketers who are in business. We are business people who happen to be marketers and communications specialists.

We stress understanding a client’s business, competitive framework and market environment as the basis for our multi-disciplinary approach. From positioning and brand-building initiatives to fully-integrated marketing programs, we combine creativity and business understanding to enhance awareness and drive results.

Located in Brooklyn, MJ Lilly Associates is certified as a Women-Owned Business (WBE) by the State of New York and the City of New York.


How We Are Different

At MJ Lilly Associates, we distinguish ourselves with:



Client Service and Integrity

We are senior, experienced professionals who not only make “sales calls” but “do the work.” Regarded for our honesty and responsiveness, we only accept those assignments where we are confident we can deliver superior results to our clients.



Strategic Intelligence and Creative Excellence

We believe that understanding our clients’ businesses is essential for all marketing – the strategic foundation for developing campaigns and all tactical initiatives. As a result, our creative expression is not just for creativity’s sake but rather as a means to move the business needle for all positioning, identity, editorial and graphic design related work across a bandwidth of communications channels to ensure ROI.



Professional Experience and Comprehensive Perspectives

Our diverse professional experiences translate into comprehensive perspectives. We are former corporate officers who managed major branding and marketing programs. We are entrepreneurs who have run our own businesses. And we are consultants with the skill set to carefully listen to our clients and recommend solutions that best meet their marketing objectives.



Pricing

The flexible nature of our consultancy allows us to develop cost-effective fees that best meet our clients’ budget scenarios. We will work on a retainer or project basis to reflect the nature of the assignment, as well as the scope of the work.
