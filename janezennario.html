Jane Zennario

Jane Zennario is a financial industry executive with deep experience in strategic marketing and product development. Passionate about capturing requirements and researching internal landscapes and external markets, Jane distills findings to articulate client needs and value propositions. Jane began her career at CS First Boston and JP Morgan, focusing on the origination, pricing, and distribution of mortgage and asset-backed securities. Subsequently, she joined Standard & Poor’s as a Vice President of strategic marketing and product development running teams to launch and market various data and analytical products targeted to institutional investors. Jane also served as the Capital Markets Product Head for Experian and a Managing Director at NatAlliance where she helped launch their investment banking efforts focused on the middle-markets. In between various corporate roles, Jane led operational and strategic marketing consulting projects at Moody’s KMV and Trepp. Jane graduated from Villanova University with a BS in Finance. She maintains her Series 7 and 63 licenses.
