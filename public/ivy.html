Ivy Asset Management




Challenge

Refresh the company’s marketing and sales platform to better reflect its market leading position and breadth of offerings in the hedge fund of funds industry.



Solution

Develop a new value proposition and product information across a bandwidth of marketing channels including presentations, brochures and the company website.

Customer Brochure

  • Articulated Ivy’s unique position as a pioneer in the hedge fund industry, global player, and successful 20+ year track record in a landscape of emerging players

  • Constructed a series of key sales messages to be utilized across all marketing and communications

  • Developed a new corporate brochure, principally aimed at high-net worth investors; the US-focused brochure was then tweaked to develop separate UK and Japanese versions

  • Drafted five product brochures focused on the principal investing categories, principally targeted to institutional investors


  • Designed a website structure and drafted all copy to emphasize Ivy’s key virtues — from experience and breadth of offerings to a disciplined investment process and superior client service

  • Provided continual updates on new product and investment process information

Result

Ivy Asset Management remains one of the pre-eminent players in the hedge fund of fund industry with assets of approximately $15 billion and strong performance in the face of difficult market conditions and a credit crunch.
